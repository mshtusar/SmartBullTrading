Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What broker should I choose?
Any broker that has good reputation under authority. Try to find broker that has been operating more than 6 years. Such as
  • IC Markets
  • Tickmil
  • FXCM
  • LiteFinance
  • Exness
What is Risk level 1?
This is the intermediate maximum risk that will be devided among trades. If any case, we breach Risk level 1, Trader will stop trading and will wait Investor's decision whether he wants to risk more or not.
What is Risk level 2?
If investor decides to take more risk, then trader will take another extra % risk to recover.
What is max. trading risk?
Max. trading risk = Risk level 1 + Risk level 2
When will I get compansation?
If total loss exceeds Max. trading risk, the extra % risk that has taken to recover, will be paid as compansation.
What is my total overall risk/loss?
Total overall risk/loss = Risk level 1 + Risk level 2 - Risk level 2(compansation amount)
